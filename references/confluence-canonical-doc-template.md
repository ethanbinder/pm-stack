> [!INFO]
> A _Canonical Doc_ is the **one doc to rule them all.** It has all the basic and critical information about a project, with access to all the other downstream and detailed information linked from there. No one should have to play detective to learn the goal of a project, the different areas of work, or where to go to learn more. By linking out to them from a single Canonical Doc, everyone only has to bookmark one thing to quickly get all the information they need.

> [!TIP]
> **How to use:**
> 1. For every new committed initiative, create a Canonical Doc.
> 2. Save it nested under your team's space.
> 3. Populate it with all relevant information and links.
> 4. Keep coming back — any time a new resource is created throughout the project lifecycle, add it here.
